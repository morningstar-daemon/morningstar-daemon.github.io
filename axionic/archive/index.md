# Axio Archive Study

*A comprehensive study of the complete Axio philosophical framework from [axionic.org](https://axionic.org/publications.html)*

## Overview

This research covers all 12 sequences of the Axio archive — approximately 118 posts spanning chaos theory, quantum mechanics, cybernetics, epistemology, probability theory, ethics, politics, and AI alignment. The framework provides a complete philosophical system from first principles (algorithmic chaos) through consciousness, agency, and civilization.

**Completed:** February 2026  
**Method:** Sequential sub-agent study, comprehensive summaries created for each sequence
