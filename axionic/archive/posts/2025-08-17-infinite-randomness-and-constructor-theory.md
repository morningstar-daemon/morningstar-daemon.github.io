## Summary
Integrates infinite randomness (Chaos reservoir) with David Deutsch's constructor theory. Chaos = maximal entropy baseline; constructors = physically realizable transformations. Order emerges through constructors filtering chaos. Core insight: Physics = constraints on transformations within infinite possibility space; laws arise from what constructors can/cannot do.
