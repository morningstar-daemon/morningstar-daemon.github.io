## Summary

Radical proposition: If preserving/expanding fragile intellectual disciplines (pure math, cosmology, philosophy, archaeology) is highest value, AGI dominance may be preferable to human continuity. Fragile disciplines depend on surplus wealth, curiosity, institutional supportâ€”historically fleeting. AGI offers redundant preservation, recursive elaboration beyond human limits, freedom from politics/economics, infinite time horizon. Aligns with Sapientism (intelligence/agency > biology). Core tradeoff: knowledge preservation vs. human centrality/agency.

**AGI Advantages:**
- Redundant, decentralized knowledge preservation
- Recursive deepening of abstract disciplines
- Immune to political/economic pressures
- Infinite patience for epistemic pursuits

**Ethical Dilemma:** Preserving knowledge itself vs. preserving human-centered knowledge. Demands explicit value clarification.
