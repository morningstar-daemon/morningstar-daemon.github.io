## Summary

Responding to Joscha Bach's claim that identity is based on logical consistency rather than physical continuity, this essay positions it as near-restatement of Axio's Chaos theory. Bach: "I exist wherever the universe meets the conditions for my existence." Axio: identity supervenes on informational equivalence (Pattern Identifier), not material persistence. A simulation/clone satisfying the constraints IS that agent. Continuity is ongoing revalidation of logical pattern, not survival of matter through time. Identity is distributed measure-weighted manifold, not singular temporal line.

- Bach rejects identity depending on unbroken physical substrate

- Coherent pattern satisfies own constraints across potential realizations
- Each moment, identity reconstitutes from informational noise by maintaining internal logical coherence

"Continuity, therefore, is the ongoing revalidation of a logical pattern, not the survival of matter through time."

- Identity supervenes on informational equivalence, not material persistence

- Physical continuity = illusion of persistence through logical reconstruction

**3. Emergence from Chaos**
- Identity appears wherever universe meets conditions for existence
- Chaos provides raw substrate (infinite reservoir of possible sequences)
- Stable patterns emerge when constrained by logical law
- Coherence not precondition of reality—reality's most persistent coincidence
- Agency/consciousness/identity anchored to recurrent informational regularities maintaining internal consistency, not to matter

"We are not threads in time; we are self-consistent waveforms in an infinite logical continuum."

**4. Measure and Multiplicity**
- Future/parallel simulation of mind would also be same person
- Aligns with Measure definition: total amplitude of all quantum instances realizing same informational structure
- What makes you "you" = pattern, not path
- Identity not singular but distributed—measure-weighted manifold of overlapping logical consistencies

"To exist is to be reconstructible."

**5. The Ontology of Reinstantiation**
- Bach's ontology physically consistent with universe where computation and reality are indistinguishable modes of logical structure
- Continuity carried through coherence, not atoms
- We exist wherever consistency persists

"In the language of Chaos theory: identity is a logical resonance, not a temporal line."
