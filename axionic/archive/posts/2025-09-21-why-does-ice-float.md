## Summary

Extends Chaos/Coherence framework by clarifying nested hierarchy of reality layers. Uses ice-floating as example: when was this property "set"? Answer: at Root PI level (Ruliad law-space), not quantum branching. Establishes four-tier architecture:

1. **Chaos Reservoir** – Raw undifferentiated potential patterns
2. **Ruliad Space (Root PIs)** – All possible law-sets (Wolfram's totality of generative rules); pre-physical modal partitioning
3. **Law-Slice (Structural PIs)** – Our specific physics (Standard Model constants, gravitation); ice-floating fixed here
4. **Everett/QBU (Event PIs)** – Contingent decoherent histories (whether *this* lake freezes this winter)

Ice floats because of our universe's law-slice constants (electron-proton mass ratio, fine-structure constant) → quantum-electronic structure → water density anomaly. This is structural consequence of Root PI selection, invariant across all branches in our universe. Conditionalism frames as: *If our constants, then ice floats.*

Philosophical payoffs: Anthropic reasoning clarified (ice-floating not contingent on branching but law-slice invariant), Conditionalism applied (all truths conditional relative to background filters), Chaos/Coherence extended (separates pre-physics Ruliad coherence from physics-level Everettian coherence).
