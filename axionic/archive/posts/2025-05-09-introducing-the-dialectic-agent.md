## Summary

Introduces Dialectic Agent: AI designed to refine/articulate ideas through structured interaction. Rooted in Plato's dialogues and Hegel's dialectical method (thesis/antithesis/synthesis).

**Functions:** (1) Structured inquiry/clarification (targeted questions uncovering assumptions/gaps), (2) Iterative conceptual refinement (repeated cycles improving coherence), (3) Idea synthesis/articulation (transforming vague thoughts into precise formulations), (4) Critical perspective (actively critiquing reasoning, suggesting alternatives).

**Example:** Developing Physics of Agency—dialectic agent provides feedback: "Your definition implicitly presupposes determinism. Clarify: classical or quantum branching causality?" Successive rounds sharpen theory.

**Benefits:** Greater conceptual clarity, rigorous thinking (explicit assumptions, logical consistency), documented intellectual growth.

**Integration:** Dialectic Session (structured dialogues), Dialectic Query (probing questions), Dialectic Synthesis (polished outcomes).
